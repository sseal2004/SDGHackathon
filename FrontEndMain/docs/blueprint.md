# **App Name**: SynchroChain

## Core Features:

- Real-time Supply Chain Data & Dashboard: Integrate real-time data from GPS, warehouse inventory, ERP, and supplier sources, providing a consolidated, live visual dashboard of the entire supply chain.
- AI-Powered Risk Prediction Tool: Utilize AI to forecast supplier delays or inconsistencies and predict potential disruptions before they occur, generating actionable reliability insights.
- Dynamic Route Optimization Tool: Simulate and optimize multi-modal logistics routes (truck, train, ship) and dynamically recommend optimized reroutes during disruptions, considering cost and time.
- Autonomous Inventory Rebalancing: Proactively reallocate stock across warehouses based on predicted demand spikes and potential delays to prevent stockouts or overstocking.
- Digital Twin & Interactive Scenario Sandbox: Create a virtual, colorful simulation of the supply chain to test 'what-if' scenarios by dragging and dropping elements, visualizing the real-time impact on risk, cost, and delivery.
- Context-Aware Smart Alerts: Deliver prioritized and actionable notifications only when intervention is truly necessary, considering factors like SKU criticality and delivery deadlines.
- Data Storage and Persistence: Securely store all integrated supply chain data, simulation results, and optimization recommendations within a MongoDB database.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant yet professional blue (#2E66FF) to convey intelligence, clarity, and precision in data-driven decision-making.
- Background color: A very light, subtle grayish-blue (#EFF1F6) provides a clean, open canvas for complex data visualizations without distraction.
- Accent color: A striking cyan (#1ACEF5) for highlights, interactive elements, and critical information, adding a dynamic and high-tech feel.
- Headline and Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, objective, and highly legible appearance, suitable for displaying detailed supply chain data and insights clearly.
- Use modern, geometric, and clear icons. Icons should be visually intuitive for quick comprehension of various data points, status indicators, and interactive components like the 'Digital Twin'.
- Design an information-rich dashboard layout that uses grid systems for logical content organization, enabling end-to-end visibility at a glance with interactive drill-down capabilities. Ensure the 'Digital Twin' sandbox offers an immersive, interactive canvas.
- Incorporate subtle, smooth transitions for data updates and filtering, and responsive animations for route simulations and 'what-if' scenario changes in the 'Digital Twin' to provide immediate visual feedback.
